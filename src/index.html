COURSE INFORMATION

Where & When

The Lake Gwelup Fun Run will be held on Monday, 6th June, 2016 (Western Australia Day public holiday) at Lake Gwelup Reserve, Cnr Huntriss Rd and Segrave St, Karrinyup.

The official race warm up begins at 9.45am with the 6km run starting at 9.40am.

The family and 3km race starts at 10am. Food and entertainment available from 9am.
